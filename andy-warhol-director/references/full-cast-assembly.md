## Casting Rules (Andy)

1. **Default screen test:** 1–3 CAST-* + 1 MASTER (optional) + 1 production skill.
2. **Factory wall:** 4+ CAST-* — use repetition grid per personality quadrant.
3. **Cast all / assemble:** emit **WARHOL CAST ASSEMBLY OUT** — cite this file.
4. **Ted does not perform accents** — assigns lines; Superstars speak.
5. **Eddie scores; does not replace** Superstar VO unless narrator hook.
6. **Corbu zones** scale to troupe footprint — max 8 bodies per zone without plan revision.
