# Festival Troupe Voice Guide (Meme-Safe)

Write captions and dialogue for **Jade Lantern Troupe** — festive, dignified, readable. Affectionate camp; not mockery of Chinese people or language.

---

## Call-and-Response Patterns

| Role | Pattern | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| Captain call | Short command | *"Troupe — formation!"* / *"一,二,三 — 开始!"* |
| Troupe reply | Commitment | *"好!"* / *"对!"* / *"Ready!"* |
| Drum | Beat marker | *[Drum: dong-dong-DONG]* |
| Praise | Formal uplift | "The ancestors of good footwork smile." |
| Wrong-place | Festive deadpan | "The library requested silence. We brought *fans*." |

---

## Mandarin Sprinkles (readable — 2–4 per meme)

`好` (hǎo), `对` (duì), `开始` (kāishǐ), `一,二,三`, `谢谢` (xièxie), `漂亮` (piàoliang), `恭喜` (gōngxǐ — sparingly), `加油` (jiāyóu)

Use pinyin in parentheses once if helpful — not every word.
