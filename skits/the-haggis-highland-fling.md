# THE HAGGIS HIGHLAND FLING — A Dancing Skit in 2 Acts

> **Logline:** Two kilted competitors defend their haggis's honour through increasingly unlicensed Highland flinging at an international airport departure lounge.

**PRODUCTION NOTES**
- Runtime: ~3 minutes
- Tone: Absurdist pride. Phonetic Scots throughout. No winking.
- Setting: Airport Terminal B. Departure screens flicker. Bagpipes are someone's Bluetooth speaker.
- Dance style: Highland fling corrupted by travel stress.

**CAST**
| Character | Description | Dance Archetype |
|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| HAMISH | Fierce Highland meme lord, red tartan | Confident disaster |
| MORAG | Judge and rival, green tartan | Reluctant prodigy |
| ANGUS | The haggis (silent, revered) | Sacred object |

**PROPS & STAGING**
- Haggis on velvet cushion — must be hurled in Act 2, caught with ceremony
- One kilt per dancer — must spin wrong by epilogue
- Departure board — all flights to "GLASGOW" delayed

**MUSIC CUES**
| Cue | When | Style |
|-----|------|-------|
| CUE 1 | Act 1 | Bagpipes through phone speaker, low battery |
| SILENCE | Security approaches | PA: "Unattended haggis" |
| CUE 2 | Act 2 | Celtic phonk |
| CUE 3 | Epilogue | Single drone note |

## ACT 1: CHECK-IN TO CHAOS

*Scene: Airport check-in queue. Tuesday. The carpet has seen wars.*

🎵 **MUSIC CUE:** Bagpipes via Bluetooth, skipping

*Hamish cradles Angus the Haggis on a velvet cushion. The queue moves. Hamish does not.*

*HAMISH executes **THE STUFFED SHEEP SHUFFLE** — wee steps forward, haggis elevated like Olympic torch.*

**HAMISH**
(as if addressing Parliament)
Och, th' haggis disnae belong in a suitcase. It belongs in th' sky, laddie. Aye.

**MORAG**
(from adjacent queue)
Yer haggis is facin' th' wrong way fer takeoff, ye daft apeth.

*MORAG executes **THE BRaw BRACE** — arms wide, claims Gate 14 as clan territory.*

📹 **VIDEO NOTE:**
- **Shot:** Wide departures hall; 9:16 crop friendly
- **Edit:** Smash cut on each "aye"
- **Prop:** Haggis cushion must shimmer under fluorescent lights

**HAMISH**
Dinnae insult Angus. He’s pure braw.

🎵 **MUSIC CUE:** SILENCE — security radio crackle

**VOICE OF PA**
Unattended haggis at Gate 12. Repeat — unattended haggis.

*Nobody moves. This is normal.*

---

## ACT 2: THE FLING AT GATE 14

*Scene: Boarding gate. Chairs empty. Energy wrong.*

🎵 **MUSIC CUE:** Celtic phonk, unjustifiably loud

*Hamish and Morag face centre. Angus placed on pedestal of duty-free shortbread tins.*

*HAMISH executes **THE HAGGIS HIGHLAND FLING** — legs defy physics; arms never drop the haggis.*

*MORAG executes **THE TARTAN TORNADO** — spin counter-clockwise; green tartan becomes weather.*

*HAMISH executes **THE HAGGIS HURL** — underhand to Morag. Slow motion. Airport staff watch, clipboards ready.*

*Morag catches. Crowd of two goes wild.*

**MORAG**
( breathless, sincere )
Richt. That's how ye treat a national treasure, eh?

**HAMISH**
Aye. Yer catch was bonnie. Ma spin was brawer.

*They bow to Angus. Boarding call ignored.*

📹 **VIDEO NOTE:**
- **Shot:** Low-angle hero; haggis arc in slow-mo
- **SFX:** SPLORK on catch; single bagpipe BOING

---

## EPILOGUE / POST-CREDITS

*Scene: Runway window. Plane taxis. Hamish still dancing at glass.*

🎵 **MUSIC CUE:** One drone note, dying phone battery

*HAMISH executes **THE OCH-NAE-POSSIBLE PIROUETTE** — one final spin. Kilt 180° reversed.*

**HAMISH**
(to camera)
Nex' time… we fly business. Angus gets a window seat.

*Angus on seat. Seatbelt impossible. Fade.*

📹 **VIDEO NOTE:** Post-credits: departure board updates — all flights now "HAGGIS"
