# THE SUPERMARKET SHUFFLE — A Dancing Skit in 3 Acts

> **Logline:** A cheerful retail associate conducts aisle inventory entirely through interpretive movement while customers treat the choreography as standard supermarket protocol.

**PRODUCTION NOTES**
- Runtime: ~4 minutes
- Tone: Absurdist physical comedy. No winking at camera.
- Setting: Fluorescent-lit suburban supermarket. Treat as historically accurate.
- Dance style: Exaggerated anime-adjacent silliness escalating into bureaucratic precision.

**CAST**
| Character | Description | Dance Archetype |
|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| YUKI | Cheerful anime-style associate in green apron | Confident disaster who becomes inexplicably good |
| DEREK | Middle-aged man buying exactly one lemon | Reluctant prodigy |
| MARGARET | Pensioner with wire basket | Silent interpreter via trolley angles |
| VOICE OF THE PA | Unseen. Exists only to interrupt | The mundane |

**PROPS & STAGING**
- Shopping trolley — Margaret's only dialogue
- Cereal box pyramid — must be toppled in Act 3 by accident, not acknowledged
- Price gun — Yuki clicks it rhythmically; it is not a percussion instrument (it is)
- Staging note: Checkout lane 4 remains closed throughout. Nobody explains why.

**MUSIC CUES**
| Cue | When | Style Description |
|-----|------|-------------------|
| CUE 1 | Act 1 open | Triumphant 80s synth implying the protagonist is about to fix the cereal aisle |
| CUE 2 | Act 2 escalation | Eurodisco with the urgency of a fire drill at a library |
| SILENCE | Act 2, mid-aisle | Music cuts. PA speaks. |
| CUE 3 | Act 3 climax | Bossa nova played at double speed by someone who has never heard bossa nova |
| CUE 4 | Epilogue | Elevator music. One note wrong. |

## ACT 1: THE CEREAL CONSULTATION

*Scene: Aisle 7 — Breakfast & Regret. Mid-morning. Fluorescent tubes hum at the frequency of mild obligation.*

🎵 **MUSIC CUE:** Triumphant 80s synth implying the protagonist is about to fix the cereal aisle.

*YUKI enters frame left, full body visible, arms at professional readiness. She has been trained for this. The training was wrong.*

*YUKI executes **THE INVENTORY FLUTTER** — wrists rotate as if counting invisible stock units with medical conviction.*

**YUKI**
(as if announcing a weather advisory)
Good morning. The porridge is to your left and slightly ahead of your emotional preparedness.

*DEREK rounds the corner with one lemon. He freezes. Not from surprise. From social protocol he cannot name.*

*DEREK executes **THE RELUCTANT PRODUCE SIDESTEP** — a single step that suggests he was always going to join.*

**DEREK**
(quietly, to the lemon)
We discussed this.

*MARGARET drifts into background, trolley angled at 47 degrees. This is her entire thesis.*

📹 **VIDEO NOTE:**
- **Shot:** Wide tracking shot, waist height, following YUKI down the aisle
- **Edit gag:** Smash cut to cereal box faces — all appear to watch
- **Wardrobe/prop:** YUKI's apron must be bright green; 9:16 vertical framing preferred

*YUKI executes **THE BUREAUCRATIC SHUFFLE** — small committed steps between shelves without moving her feet from the linoleum.*

**YUKI**
Every great journey begins with a single aisle.

🎵 **MUSIC CUE:** Music stops.

*Silence. The PA crackles.*

**VOICE OF THE PA**
(as if recalling a mild disappointment from 1987)
Attention shoppers. We have lost a child. Again. He answers to Kevin.

*Beat. Nobody stops dancing.*

**DEREK**
That's actually very motivating.

## ACT 2: THE DAIRY INCIDENT

*Scene: Aisle 12 — Chilled Goods & Quiet Judgment. The same morning. Condensation on glass doors like polite tears.*

🎵 **MUSIC CUE:** Eurodisco with the urgency of a fire drill at a library.

*YUKI spins once near the yoghurt section. She knocks nothing over. This is the most impressive thing anyone has ever done.*

*DEREK executes **THE LEMON-LED PIVOT** — slow 270° turn maintaining eye contact with a two-litre milk.*

**DEREK**
(as if defending a thesis)
I've always said dairy is a lifestyle.

*MARGARET's trolley executes **THE TROLLEY DECLARATION** — a wide arc that communicates agreement without participation.*

*A STOCKBOY enters. He carries one box. He watches. He does not intervene. This is company policy.*

**STOCKBOY**
(never breaking stride)
Aisle twelve's looking tight today.

*He exits. He was never truly there.*

📹 **VIDEO NOTE:**
- **Shot:** Medium orbit around YUKI as arm movements become increasingly unnecessary
- **Edit gag:** Slow-motion on Derek's lemon at the exact wrong moment
- **Wardrobe/prop:** Cold blue light from dairy fridges; breath visible on glass

*YUKI executes **THE SYNERGY SLIDE** — slides three feet across linoleum while the price gun clicks in 4/4 time.*

**YUKI**
Teamwork makes the dream work.

🎵 **MUSIC CUE:** SILENCE

**VOICE OF THE PA**
Fish fingers. Aisle nine. Emotionally available.

---

## ACT 3: THE CHECKOUT TRIUMPH

*Scene: Checkout Lane 4 — Closed, but spiritually open. Afternoon now, or still morning. Time is not the issue.*

🎵 **MUSIC CUE:** Bossa nova played at double speed by someone who has never heard bossa nova.

*YUKI, DEREK, and MARGARET converge at the closed lane as if summoned by an appointment none of them made.*

*All three execute **THE TRIUMPHANT CHECKOUT POSE** — arms raised. Trolley angled. Lemon elevated. Cereal box pyramid collapses behind them.*

*Nobody turns around.*

*YUKI executes **THE CONFUSED FLAMINGO** — one leg up, total sincerity, zero balance. She holds it. She believes in it.*

**YUKI**
(as if closing a successful quarter)
Thank you for shopping with us. Your feedback is important.

**DEREK**
(to the lemon)
We did it.

*MARGARET's trolley emits a single squeak. This counts as applause.*

📹 **VIDEO NOTE:**
- **Shot:** Low-angle hero shot, then pull back to wide
- **Edit gag:** Freeze frame on YUKI mid-flamingo; credits roll over continued motion
- **Wardrobe/prop:** Cereal boxes scattered like fallen soldiers; price gun still clicking

🎵 **MUSIC CUE:** CUE 3 climax — full chaos, 10 seconds, hard cut to silence

---

## EPILOGUE / POST-CREDITS

*Scene: Same supermarket. Night. One security light. YUKI is still dancing. The store closed four hours ago.*

🎵 **MUSIC CUE:** Elevator music. One note wrong.

*YUKI executes **THE AFTER-HOURS COMPLIANCE WALTZ** — three steps forward, one step into a display of tinned beans.*

**YUKI**
(whispered, sincere)
See you tomorrow.

*She bows to the self-checkout machine. It does not beep.*

📹 **VIDEO NOTE:**
- **Shot:** Static wide. Hold. Do not explain.
- **Final frame:** Cut to black on the wrong beat. Mid-credits sting optional.
