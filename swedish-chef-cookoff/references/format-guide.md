# Swedish Chef Cook-Off — Format Guide

Full formatting spec for production-ready cook-off challenge scripts.

---

## Document Header

```markdown
# [CHALLENGE TITLE] — A Swedish Chef Cook-Off in [N] Rounds

> **Logline:** One sentence. Deadpan. Sounds like a Michelin inspector having a breakdown.

**CHALLENGE BRIEF**
- **Theme:** [Absurd hook]
- **Runtime:** ~[N] minutes
- **Tone:** Muppet Show kitchen. No food safety. Maximum bork.
- **Setting:** [Location — must be wrong for cooking]
- **Stakes:** [Ridiculous prize — e.g. "Golden Meatball of Questionable Origin"]

**CONTESTANTS**
| Chef | Variant | Archetype | Signature Move |
|------|---------|-----------|----------------|
| [NAME] | Swedish / Norwegian / etc. | Purist / Innovator / Saboteur | **THE [TECHNIQUE]** |

**MYSTERY BASKET**
| # | Ingredient | Notes |
|---|------------|-------|
| 1 | [Item] | [Alive? Sentient? Wrong?] |
| ... | ... | ... |
| N | Flörgen | Undefined. Do not explain. |

**JUDGES & SCORING**
| Judge | Personality | Biased Toward |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| [NAME] | Grumpy / Enthusiastic / Confused | Loud noises / Meatballs / Fire |

**Scoring categories:** Bork Factor | Flörgen Integrity | Chaos Commitment | Meatball Symmetry

**CHAOS EVENTS SCHEDULE**
| When | Event |
|------|-------|
| Round 1, min 3 | [Event] |
| Round 2, min 7 | [Event] |
| Judging | [Event] |
```

## Round Block Pattern

```markdown
---

## ROUND [N]: [ROUND TITLE]

*Scene: [Location]. [Time]. [Atmospheric detail — nature documentary voice.]*

🎵 **MUSIC CUE:** [Describe — e.g. "Polka that implies imminent structural damage"]

**HOST**
(BBC calm)
And so our chefs begin the [phase]. They have [time]. They need [wrong thing].

*[CHEF] performs **THE [TECHNIQUE NAME]** — [deadpan description.]*

**[CHEF NAME]**
[Swedish Chef speak]
*[optional subtitle]*

*[Cooking chaos stage direction. Props fly. Nobody ducks.]*

**SABOTAGE BEAT**
*[What goes wrong "accidentally." Chef thanks the saboteur.]*

**[SECOND CHEF]**
[Response — escalates, never de-escalates]

📹 **VIDEO NOTE:**
- **Shot:** [Overhead chaos cam / Close-up on wrong ingredient / Dutch angle on fire]
- **SFX:** [BOING / SPLORK / etc.]
- **Prop:** [Flying spoon / Conscious whisk / etc.]
- **Puppet note:** [Wire visible OK — Muppet aesthetic]

🎵 **MUSIC CUE:** [Change / Stop for silence gag]

*[SILENCE — mundane line from host or judge]*

**HOST**
The timer reads [wrong time]. Nobody checks.

---
```

## Judging Block

```markdown
---

## JUDGING & VERDICT

*Scene: Judging table. Dishes smolder. Judges have not been briefed. This is fine.*

**JUDGE 1**
(Sniffs dish that is clearly not food)
A bold use of... texture.

**JUDGE 2**
I detect notes of [wrong thing]. Exquisite.

| Chef | Bork | Flörgen | Chaos | Meatball | Total |
|------|------|---------|-------|----------|-------|
| [Name] | [N] | [N] | [N] | [N] | [Wrong math] |

**HOST**
And the winner is — *[pause]* — everyone. Or no one. The Golden [Prize] goes to [wrong recipient].

**ALL CHEFS**
Bork bork bork!

---
```

## Epilogue

```markdown
---

## EPILOGUE

*Scene: Kitchen. [Time later]. Fire optional. Cooking continues.*

🎵 **MUSIC CUE:** Elevator polka. One accordion key stuck.

**SWEDISH CHEF**
(alone, to camera)
Nex' time... ve try de flörgen again.

*A duck applauds. Credits roll over active fire.*

📹 **VIDEO NOTE:**
- **Shot:** Static wide. Hold. Cut to black on wrong beat.
- **Post-credits:** Chef still cooking. Timer still wrong.

---
```

## Annotated Mini-Example

Condensed reference — remix structure, not content.

```markdown
# THE MEATBALL TRIALS — A Swedish Chef Cook-Off in 2 Rounds

> **Logline:** Three Nordic chefs compete to create the roundest meatball using only items from a submarine's lost-and-found.

**MYSTERY BASKET:** Rubber duck, flörgen, one (1) dignity, tinned regret, live accordion, graphite

---

## ROUND 1: THE PANICKEN PREP

*Scene: Submarine galley. 2 PM or 200 leagues — time is damp.*

**SWEDISH CHEF**
Bork bork bork! De meaty-boll she needa de roundness!

*Performs **THE SPHERIFICATION PANIC** — shapes something that is not a meatball with total confidence.*

*[subtitle: That is a buoy]*

**SABOTAGE BEAT:** Norwegian Nils "helps" by adding more flörgen. Both chefs salute.

📹 **VIDEO NOTE:** Overhead cam; BOING on every stir; puppet wires visible on duck

---

## JUDGING & VERDICT

**JUDGE**
I award full marks for acoustics.

Winner: The accordion. Chefs applaud anyway.

---

## EPILOGUE

**SWEDISH CHEF**
Vell... nex' year ve get it righter.

*Cut to black. Sizzling continues.*
```
